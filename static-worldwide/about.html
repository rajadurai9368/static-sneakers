Est. 2021 · Lisbon → Worldwide

Quiet by design.

STATIC started with a simple frustration: sneakers had gotten loud. Louder logos, louder colorways, louder marketing. We wanted the opposite — a shoe that felt considered, wore soft, and let the person do the talking.

The Manifesto

We make shoes for the ones who’d rather be felt than heard.

Every STATIC pair begins with a tone, not a trend. Wearable neutrals — bone, sand, clay, frost — cut with a single accent that only reveals itself up close. No screaming branding. No seasonal noise. Just a quiet silhouette built to be worn every day, for years.

We release in small, deliberate drops. Forty to two hundred pairs. One release, no restock. When it’s gone, it’s gone — because scarcity should mean something, and because we’d rather make the next thing than reprint the last.

What we stand on

Three things we refuse to compromise.

01

Considered materials

Cream suedes, recycled knits, gum and natural rubber soles. Everything is chosen to age well and feel good on foot from the first wear — no breaking-in required.

02

Deliberate scarcity

Limited runs, zero restocks. We’d rather sell out in 48 hours than flood the market. It keeps the work honest and the drops meaningful.

03

Quiet confidence

No oversized logos, no hype for hype’s sake. The design does the work. You’ll notice a STATIC pair by the way it moves, not the way it shouts.

How we got here

A short, quiet history.

2021

A garage in Lisbon

Two friends, one sample maker, and a stubborn idea: a soft street sneaker with nothing to prove. The first forty pairs sold to friends of friends.

2022

Drop 00 — “Shadow”

Our origin colorway. Triple black, blackout sole, no branding on the outside. It sold out in a weekend and put STATIC on the map.

2024

The waitlist hits 200k

Word travelled the way we hoped — quietly, person to person. We moved to a small studio, kept the runs limited, and never restocked a single pair.

2026

Drop 01 — “Rust Series”

Warm tones, cold drop. Our most considered release yet — and the reason you’re here. Same principles, sharper execution.
