The Studio

Small team, few pairs.

Six people make every STATIC drop — from the first sketch to the pair on your feet. No agencies, no middle layers. Just a studio that would rather do less, better.

MO

Maya Okonkwo

Founder · Creative Director

Started STATIC in a Lisbon garage. Sets the tone for every drop and still signs off on the last sample before it ships.

DR

Devon Reyes

Head of Design

Owns the silhouette. Obsessed with the millimetre — the exact curve of a toe box, the weight of a lace tip.

IS

Ilya Sorokin

Materials & Development

Sources the suedes, knits and rubbers. If it doesn’t age well, it doesn’t make the cut.

NH

Nadia Haddad

Brand & Community

Keeps the waitlist warm and the story quiet. Believes the best marketing is a shoe worth talking about.

TM

Theo Marsh

Operations

Makes the drops actually land — production, logistics, and the promise that a sold-out pair reaches you fast.

RT

Rin Takahashi

Photography · Art Direction

Shoots every campaign on foot, in real light. The Move Different series is hers, frame for frame.
